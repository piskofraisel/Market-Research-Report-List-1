<p><p>Parallel sorting robots can be categorized into two main market types: Two-Axis and Multi-Axis. Two-Axis robots operate on a simple system, moving along two perpendicular axes to sort items, ideal for straightforward tasks with limited complexity. In contrast, Multi-Axis robots utilize multiple joints and axes, providing greater flexibility and precision for intricate sorting operations. This advanced functionality allows them to handle diverse products and adapt to various layouts, catering to industries requiring high efficiency and customization in sorting processes.</p></p>
