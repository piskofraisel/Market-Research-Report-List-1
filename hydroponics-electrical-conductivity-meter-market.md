<p><p>The hydroponics electrical conductivity meter market includes two primary types: completely waterproof electrical conductivity meters and waterproof probe electrical conductivity meters. Completely waterproof meters are designed for full submersion, offering protection against moisture and humidity, making them ideal for wet environments. In contrast, waterproof probe meters feature probes that can withstand water exposure while the main unit remains protected, allowing for flexible and convenient measurements. Both types play a crucial role in managing nutrient levels in hydroponic systems.</p></p>
