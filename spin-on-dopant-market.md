<p><p>The Spin-on Dopant market is segmented into P-type and N-type categories based on the type of semiconductor doping agent used. P-type dopants introduce holes by adding elements like boron, enhancing positive charge carriers in semiconductor materials primarily for applications in electronics and photovoltaics. Conversely, N-type dopants introduce electrons by incorporating elements like phosphorus, increasing negative charge carriers. Both types are essential for creating p-n junctions in devices such as transistors, diodes, and solar cells, enabling advanced electronic functionalities.</p></p>
