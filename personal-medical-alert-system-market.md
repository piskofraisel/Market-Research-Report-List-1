<p><p>The Personal Medical Alert System market is divided into two main types: fixed and mobile systems. They often provide a base unit with a wearable pendant for convenience. In contrast, mobile systems are portable and allow users to carry the alert device, enabling them to call for help from anywhere, enhancing safety for active individuals outside the home.</p></p>
