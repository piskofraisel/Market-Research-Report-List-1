<p><p>The multichip modules (MCM) market is categorized into three main types: laminated, stacked, and others. Laminated MCMs feature multiple chips bonded together using layers of insulating materials, enhancing thermal management and electrical performance. Stacked MCMs involve vertically stacking chips to minimize footprint and improve connectivity, ideal for high-density applications. The 'others' category encompasses various innovative designs and hybrid solutions that combine characteristics of both laminated and stacked types, catering to specialized requirements in advanced electronic applications.</p></p>
