<p><ul><li>Non-Contact</li><li>Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The embedded temperature sensor market is divided into two main types: non-contact and contact sensors. Non-contact sensors utilize infrared technology to measure temperature from a distance, making them ideal for applications where the target is moving or difficult to access. In contrast, contact sensors directly touch the object to measure temperature, providing precise readings for stable environments. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, including automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation, catering to varying measurement needs and operating conditions.</p></p>
