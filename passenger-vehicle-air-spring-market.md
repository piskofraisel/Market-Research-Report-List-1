<p><p>The passenger vehicle air spring market comprises three main types: convoluted, sleeve, and rolling lobe. Convoluted air springs feature a flexible, folded design that provides excellent load support and adaptability. Sleeve air springs consist of a cylindrical rubber sleeve, offering a lightweight option with good stability and comfort. Rolling lobe air springs use a configuration that allows for consistent performance under varying loads, enhancing ride quality. Each type caters to specific vehicle designs and performance requirements, enhancing overall driving experiences.</p></p>
