<p><p>The farm animal external medicine market is categorized into external and internal use segments. External use involves treatments applied directly to the skin or mucous membranes, such as topical antibacterial ointments, antiseptics, and wound care products. In contrast, the internal use market focuses on medications administered systemically, such as oral antibiotics, anti-parasitics, or vaccines. Both segments are crucial for maintaining the health and productivity of livestock, addressing various ailments while ensuring animal welfare and food safety.</p></p>
