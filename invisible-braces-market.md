<p><p>The invisible braces market consists of three main types: clear aligners, ceramic braces, and lingual braces. Clear aligners are transparent, removable trays that gradually shift teeth into alignment, offering a discreet option. Ceramic braces use tooth-colored brackets and wires, blending well with natural teeth while providing the effectiveness of traditional braces. Lingual braces are placed behind the teeth, making them virtually invisible to others. Together, these options cater to individuals seeking orthodontic treatment without the visibility of metal braces.</p></p>
