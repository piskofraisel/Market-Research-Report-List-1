<p><strong>The Satellite Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ground risk</li><li>Satellite risk</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The satellite insurance market comprises two main types: ground risk and satellite risk. Ground risk insurance covers the physical assets associated with satellite operations on Earth, such as launch vehicles, facilities, and payloads during ground handling. In contrast, satellite risk insurance addresses the risks faced by the satellite once it is in orbit, including technical failures, debris collision, and loss of operational capacity. Together, these markets provide comprehensive coverage for satellite operations from launch to active service.</p></p>
