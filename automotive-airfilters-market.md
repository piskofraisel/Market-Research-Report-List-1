<p><p>The automotive air filters market encompasses various types designed to enhance vehicle air quality. Particle filters capture dust, pollen, and other airborne particulates, ensuring clean air intake for the engine and interior. Activated carbon filters further aid in filtering by absorbing gases, odors, and harmful pollutants, improving cabin air quality. Electrostatic filters use charged fibers to attract and trap particles, offering efficiency in removing fine dust. Each type plays a vital role in optimizing engine performance and passenger comfort.</p></p>
