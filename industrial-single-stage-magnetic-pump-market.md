<p><strong>The Industrial Single-stage Magnetic Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Shaft</li><li>Rotating Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial single-stage magnetic pump market is divided into two main types based on shaft configuration: stationary shaft and rotating shaft. Stationary shaft magnetic pumps utilize a fixed internal shaft, allowing for efficient sealing and reduced leakage, making them suitable for handling volatile liquids. In contrast, rotating shaft magnetic pumps feature a moving central shaft, which can handle higher flow rates and pressures. Both types leverage magnetic coupling to eliminate direct contact between the pump and motor, enhancing performance and reliability.</p></p>
