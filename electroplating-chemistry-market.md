<p><strong>The Electroplating Chemistry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pretreatment Agent</li><li>Electroplating Additive</li><li>Post-treatment Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electroplating chemistry market is divided into three main types: pretreatment agents, electroplating additives, and post-treatment agents. Pretreatment agents prepare surfaces for plating by cleaning and activating substrates, ensuring better adhesion. Electroplating additives enhance the electroplating process by improving deposit quality, control, and efficiency, influencing factors like brightness and hardness. Post-treatment agents are applied after plating to enhance corrosion resistance, durability, and aesthetics of the coating, thus ensuring a superior finish and extending the lifespan of the plated items.</p></p>
