<p><p>Vacuum skin packaging films are essential for preserving food quality and extending shelf life. The market is primarily segmented into flexible films and semi-rigid films. Flexible films are adaptable and conform to the product's shape, providing a tight seal that minimizes air and moisture exposure. In contrast, semi-rigid films offer greater structural support while still maintaining a vacuum seal, making them ideal for heavier or irregularly shaped items. Both types enhance freshness, reduce spoilage, and improve product presentation.</p></p>
