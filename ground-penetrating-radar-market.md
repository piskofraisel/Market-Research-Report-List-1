<p><p>The Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) market is segmented into two primary types: handheld and cart-based systems. Handheld GPR units are portable and user-friendly, designed for quick scans and localized surveys, making them ideal for small-scale applications such as utility detection and archaeological investigations. In contrast, cart-based GPR systems are mounted on wheeled platforms, offering enhanced mobility and stability for larger surveys. These are typically used in infrastructure assessments, geological investigations, and extensive subsurface mapping projects, combining efficiency with advanced data collection capabilities.</p></p>
